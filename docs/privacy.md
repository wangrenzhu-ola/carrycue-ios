# CarryCue privacy and safety boundary

- Practice cards persist only in the app's local Application Support directory.
- Users are instructed to use pseudonymous labels and omit names, contact information, record numbers, diagnoses, and audio.
- Optional Kimi drafting sends only the target phrase, observed response, cue level, and practice goal after per-card consent.
- Generated text is editable, unapproved by default, and cannot become an approved handoff without explicit clinician action.
- The full manual create, edit, approve, reopen, revise, and delete workflow remains available without cloud AI.
- The app declares no tracking and requests no camera, microphone, photo, contacts, location, or ATT permission.
